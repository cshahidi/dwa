Help on Using Calculator

Directions

Enter all values in integer form. All decimal values are rounded to next lowest integer.

Glossary

After Repair Value: Market price of property after renovation.

Profit Goal: Profit envisioned by investor. Is an expense in this context.

Commission: Paid to agent upon resale of property. Usually 6%, but can vary.

Closing Costs: Legal and other fees upon purchase (in) and sale (out)

Principal & Interest: Mortgage payments envisioned for 6 months. This value is 0 if the purchase is all cash. If not, user can use the "Subtotal" figure displayed after the Calculate button is clicked as the "Property Price" and use the Mortgage Calculator at http://www.trulia.com/mortgage-calculators/ to find the monthly mortgage. This value must then be multiplied by 6 to give a six month value to plug into the "Principal & Interest" field.
